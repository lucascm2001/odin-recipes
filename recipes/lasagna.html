Lasagna

Description

Tender sheets of pasta, a creamy ricotta cheese filling, and a rich meaty tomato sauce are all topped with more cheese and baked until browned and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Beef and Sausage
  • Garlic
  • Egg
  • Mozzarella
  • Lasagna
  • Onion
  • Tomato Paste
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Prepare meat sauce by cooking the meat with onion and garlic. Drain well, add pasta sauce and simmer.
  3. Combine cheese mixture in a bowl.
  4. Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles an dbake until browned and bubbly.
